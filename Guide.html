Beginner's Guide

Welcome! We're excited to have you.

1. Use

W

or

Space

to fire the main engines.


2. Use

A

and

D

to rotate your ship.


3. The

breadcrumbs

indicate your ship's trajectory.


4. Use

Q

/

E

or

mouse wheel

to zoom the camera.

Tip: frequently change zoom to better understand your ship's position in the universe.


5. Press

N

to select a station to navigate to.

When the target station is in view, it will be circled in a white dashed line.

When it is out of view, a pointer will indicates the direction to the station.


6. To dock at a station, match its orbit and press

Shift

.

Tip: your ship AI will tell you if your relative velocity is too high, or the distance is too great, for you to dock.

6. While docked, use the station market to buy and sell goods.

7. Buy goods for cheap, and sell them at another station that will pay a higher price for them.

Tip: shorter journeys are generally better.

8. Profit!
